Meet the Team!

This is our culture

Our goal is to give you as many unique and delicious recipes as possible for those who are limited to certain restrictions whether it is dietary or voluntary! We want to give you as many healthy recipes as possible but we also believe in a good cheat dessert or meal as well, thats how we came up with our name Healthy- ish Eats. We strive for the 80% healthy 20% treat lifestyle! And who says eating healthy isnt just as yummy! Check out our new video with vegan fall recipes to help be inspired!          

Who we are

Healthy-ish Eats is proudly going on five years of delivering some of the best vegan, vegetarian and pescatarian food recipes to you! We started our business because of the lack of food options for a lot of non meat and dairy eaters. We love keeping our viewers full and happy!
